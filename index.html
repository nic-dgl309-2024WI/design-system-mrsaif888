Clusia

Clusia plants, native to the Americas, feature thick, leathery leaves and thrive in tropical climates. Popular for landscaping and indoor decoration, they're low-maintenance, drought-tolerant, and ideal for privacy screens.

Fittonia

Fittonia, or nerve plant, originating from South America, boasts vibrant foliage with intricate vein patterns. Thriving in low light and humid conditions, it's a popular choice for indoor gardening, adding color

Succulent

Succulents are water-retaining plants known for their thick, fleshy leaves and stems. Adapted to arid conditions, they come in various shapes, sizes, and colors, making them popular choices for indoor and outdoor gardens.

Dracaena

Dracaena, a genus of ornamental plants, features long, sword-shaped leaves. These low-maintenance plants are popular for indoor decoration, offering a touch of greenery with their vibrant foliage.

Lavender

Lavender, an aromatic herb native to the Mediterranean, boasts fragrant purple flowers and narrow, silver-green leaves. It's prized for its soothing scent and diverse uses in cooking, aromatherapy, and skincare.

Hydrangeas

Hydrangeas are deciduous shrubs known for their large clusters of colorful flowers. With their ability to change color based on soil pH, they add beauty and charm to gardens and floral arrangements.
